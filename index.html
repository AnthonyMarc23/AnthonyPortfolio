Welcome to my Portfolio Page! It's been a long journey for myself, jumping between dozens of artistic disciplines including writing, drawing, composing, and even coding. In that time, I've enjoyed learning the process of creating my own games in all of their forms, with video games being the most ambitious.

In the mean time, take a look around, and enjoy the retro feels of my portfolio page.
